📈 Watchlist Weekly

1. $TSLA – Tesla
The king of EV. Everyone has an opinion, and that’s the edge.

Why I’m watching: Key chart levels in play. AI + robotaxis keep narrative strong despite delivery concerns.

Sentiment: 🔁 Constant — retail, funds, and Twitter all watching

My take: If it breaks resistance with volume, I’m in. Holding above support = confidence.

2. $PLTR – Palantir
The black box of government AI — mysterious but powerful.

Why I’m watching: AI contracts keep piling in. Palantir’s unique public + private position is rare.

Sentiment: 🧠 Divisive — retail bullish, institutions still unsure

My take: Consolidating well. If it holds above 20, it could be prepping for a strong move into summer.

3. $SOFI – SoFi Technologies
Not a bank? Not a fintech? A bit of both, and that’s the play.

Why I’m watching: SoFi’s user growth is impressive and its digital-first model appeals to Gen Z and millennials. Lending + investing = sticky revenue.

Sentiment: 💡 Under-the-radar — popular with retail but still under-owned institutionally

My take: Price action is coiling. If macro sentiment lifts, SoFi could lead small caps up.

4. LSEG.L – London Stock Exchange Group
The backbone of UK finance.

Why I’m watching: LSEG’s strategic pivot to data analytics and its acquisition of Refinitiv sets it apart. Earnings in focus.

Sentiment: 🏦 Institutional comfort — conservative growth, stable returns

My take: Good long-term hold, might add if it dips near support zone on volume.

5. HL.L – Hargreaves Lansdown
UK’s biggest investment platform.

Why I’m watching: A bellwether for UK retail sentiment. Any rebound in market participation should show here first.

Sentiment: 📉 Mixed — concern over retail flows, but solid fundamentals

My take: Watching earnings reaction closely. Breakout above recent high could lead to trend reversal.
